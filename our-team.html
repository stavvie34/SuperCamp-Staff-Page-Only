GET TO KNOW OUR SUPERCAMP TEAM

Each and every staff member at SuperCamp, from those at our headquarters in Oceanside, CA to our Facilitators around the world, want the best for your child.

A Note From Our Founder

I founded SuperCamp in 1982 to help students learn how to learn and reshape how they live their lives. Now, with over 78,000 graduates and programs held in over 20 countries – delivered in English, Mandarin, Spanish and Korean – we also work with thousands of schools and educators through Quantum Learning Education. Foundational to all our programs are the 8 Keys of Excellence. We love to hear from all those who attended SuperCamp, including the growing number of graduates who now have children of their own who have gone to SuperCamp.

Our Training

 

4:1 Ratio

Our student to staff ratio enables us to give your child the attention they deserve and require for success.

 

Extensive Training

Our facilitators participate in more than 150 hours of extensive training in the proprietary Quantum Learning system.

 

Hundreds of Applications/Year

We are able to hand pick the best candidates to ensure your child is constantly surrounded by positive, encouraging role models.

 

Rigorous Selection Process

Only the most qualified, committed, and caring candidates are chosen to be part of the SuperCamp team.

Our Headquarters Team

These folks work hard to make sure everything behind the scenes run smoothly. From staffing to curriculum, all of our staff works with the same goal in mind: to create the best possible experience for your child. And it shows!
