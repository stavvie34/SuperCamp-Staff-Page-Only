SuperCamp Facilitators bring their depth of knowledge of the curriculum, systems thinking, and leadership skills to create the culture of camp. Through their understanding of youth and their behavior, they are able to mentor both campers and staff. Facilitators possess the following qualities:

Professional

Credible

Collaborative

Articulate

Positive Communicator

Masterful Teacher

Personal

Resourceful

Confident

Engaging

Passionate

Lifelong Learner

Job Description

Instruct

Curriculum

Instruct Quantum Learning and Life Skills curriculum to the large group (up to 150 people) and debrief large and small group discussions.

Facilitation

Create engaging and dynamic academic course room sessions to facilitate our curriculum.

Model

Engage with students and model the expectation of active participation in all sessions.

Activities

Lead purposeful activities and games to energize, mobilize, redirect, or enroll students.

Parent Workshop

Lead an interactive and informative 2-hour Parent Workshop.

Mentor

Communication

Model safe and healthy communication and behavior for all students and staff.

Support

Provide resources and encouragement to Team Leaders for effective support of their campers.

Success

Participate in daily meetings to align, course correct, and prepare for the daily success of camp.

Feedback

Give and receive coaching and feedback to improve and maintain the quality of the program.

Support

Quality

Work with the entire site team to assure the quality delivery of all curriculum.

Impeccability

Set up and maintain the impeccability of all course rooms and meeting spaces.

Social Media

Participate in the taking of pictures, video, and posting of social media for Grad Support.

Preparation

Prepare all materials needed to create a safe and success driven learning environment.

Assistance

Provide context and assistance to any other role in delivering excellent customer service.

Able to memorize and deliver large amounts of curriculum with accuracy.
