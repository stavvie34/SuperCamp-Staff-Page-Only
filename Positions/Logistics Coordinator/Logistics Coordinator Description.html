SuperCamp Logistics Coordinators are responsible for ensuring that all logistical systems are in place, as well as keeping track of all the logistical duties. Logistics Coordinators possess the following qualities and talents; they are the reason people count on them to create and maintain professional, impeccable, well-organized environments at camp:

Timely

Energetic

Job Description

Before Camp

Training

Participate in training conference calls (2-3 throughout May-June).

Manual

Thoroughly read and review the Logistics Coordinator manual.

During Camp

Supplies

Direct the unpacking of camp supplies (from storage unit to site).

Prepare

Ensure supplies for each day are prepared well in advance.

Set-Up

Manage the physical set-up of SuperCamp spaces (Mainroom and Courseroom)

Organize

Organize and maintain supplies of camp in drawer systems.

Inventory

Complete pre-camp inventory and appropriately distribute camp supplies.

Impeccablility

Maintain impeccability of SuperCamp environment.

Arrival/Departure

Support office staff with preparations for camper arrival day and departure day.

Outdoor Adventure Day

Assist with logistics of Outdoor Adventure Day.

Assignments

Manage and organize TL assignments and afternoon activity sign ups.

Facilitate

Facilitate logistics portions of staff meetings.

Camp Breakdown

Inventory

Complete post-camp inventory and communicate next year logistical needs to Oceanside.

Supplies

Oversee the packing of camp supplies alongside the office team.

Storage

Direct the storage of all supplies in designated storage unit.
